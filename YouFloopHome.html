Why I love Youtube

Unlike other social media platforms , Youtube in a sense has always been for people to express themselves through video no matter how much of an amatuer they are. In a sense anyone could be the next big thing but only the people who actively put hours into there video's reach the fame that is seen throughtout the youtube community. Youtube has evolved throughtout the years bringing toghether people who without youtube would not have have known each other. This is aparent by the Anual YoutubeRewind© that youtube posts to show off there incredibly diverse creators that make the site what it is!

Why I choose these videos

While Im a sucker for funny quick punch line animations , I know that , not everyone is , so before warding off people with just cartoons I tried to vary the things I put on the main page. Of course there are a few repeats of type of video's, but during 2016 youtube has influxed with "Drama-related" video's to the point where its been pointed out as a problem. I hope you enjoy some of these video's even if you are to much of an intelectual. There will be video's througout the site that are thought provoking or just plain random ass funny, and I hope you enjoy them just as much as I did.

Downsides to Youtube

No social media platform is perfect , as shown by Facebooks horrific copyRight policy or the clutter of shit talking between celeberties on Twitter and on and on. But the gradual shift to length based content to maxamize ad revenue not somewhere like a site like Youtube should be heading. This large change can be felt as content on youtube especially the "Gaming community" no longer have video's shorter than 10 minutes as Youtube directly encourages this
