Early Childhood

I was born in a country called El Salvador located in the central America, and to be honest since I moved when I was 6 to the U.S I don't really remember much from the 6 years I was there aside from the taste ofPupusa's. But I do remember having the luxury to go to a private german School in El Salvador called:WALTE which I eventually left, because while attending there I ended up cutting off a piece of my ear, requiring me to get plastic surgery and having a surgeon sew it back together.(*--In a country like El Salvador they sometimes play by the different rule, compared to the US. Whereas in the Us , they would have a large lawsuit in El Salvador all they had to do is kick you out and threaten you , if you go to the courts--*).

Moving to America

I have been to certain American cities for short instances when I was 6 when like the time I flew with my mom to San Francisco. I remember vividly the first time I saw snow there. I freaked out, seeing the shower of these crystals that would disintegrate if your try to hold them.(*--since being a kid from a place where its normal for the temperature to be 100℉ and the idea of snow just reminded me of Christmas to much--*) But, aside from that America was and all that came with it was something completely new to me.(*--That included English, since at the time I only spoke Spanish--*)
